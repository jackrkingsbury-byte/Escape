Questions

The things owners ask first

Is it safe? Can it break my store?
No. StoreBrief asks for read-only access to your orders. It has no ability to change products, prices, inventory, or payouts — it can only look, count, and explain. Nothing it does touches your store or your customers.
Do I need to understand analytics?
That's the whole point — no. If you can read a WhatsApp from a friend, you can read your StoreBrief. It's written in plain English, with the one thing to do this week spelled out.
Will the numbers be right?
Every figure is computed directly from your Shopify orders — never estimated or made up. It even drops test orders, which Shopify hides from its own reports, so your brief matches your real takings to the rand.
What if I had a quiet week with no sales?
You still get a brief — an honest one. "No sales this week — here's one thing to try." No fake cheerfulness, no hiding it. Knowing is better than guessing.
What happens when the free trial ends?
It simply stops — nothing to cancel, and since there's no card on file you can never be charged by surprise. If the briefs made you money, you grab a founding plan and they keep coming.
