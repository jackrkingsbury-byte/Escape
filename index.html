A note from the person who built ESCAPE

I'm not a coach or a guru, and I won't pretend I've got life all figured out.

I built ESCAPE because I was the person it's for — losing whole evenings to the scroll, feeling behind everyone online, promising myself "tomorrow," then doing it all again.

What pulled me out wasn't motivation or some 5am routine. It was doing one small, slightly scary thing a day, and letting them stack up. So I turned what worked into something I could hand to anyone stuck in the same loop.

If one part of it gets you off the couch and into your life, it did its job.
