How it works

One loop: watch, do, grow.

GROW is a tiny, private, offline planner. Everything lives in this browser — no account, no server. It pairs a fast idea planner with a long-horizon growth tracker so today's small actions visibly build something over 15 years.

📺

1 · Capture

Drop in a video to watch or a thing to do. Tag it, set a due date, and point it at a growth area it feeds.

✅

2 · Finish

Drag it across the board or hit done. Finish a video and jot a quick rating + takeaway. Feeding areas auto-log a nutrient.

🌱

3 · Feed

Each growth area is a 15-year grid with a live cumulative chart and a pace projection: ahead, on track, or behind.

📈

4 · Compound

You can't sprint 15 years — but you can add one nutrient today. The projection shows where that pace lands you.
