How it works

One loop: watch, do, grow.

GROW is a tiny, private, offline planner. Everything lives in this browser — no account, no server. It pairs a fast idea planner with a long-horizon growth tracker so today's small actions visibly build something over 15 years.

📺

1 · Capture

Drop in a video to watch or a thing to do. Tag it, prioritise it, and point it at a growth area it feeds.

✅

2 · Finish

Mark it done. If it feeds a growth area, GROW automatically logs a nutrient for you — the planner and the tracker stay in sync.

🌱

3 · Feed

Each growth area is a 15-year grid. Every nutrient darkens the current year's cell and pushes you toward your target.

📈

4 · Compound

You can't sprint 15 years — but you can add one nutrient today. The grid shows the streak of years you kept showing up.
