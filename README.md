# ⚡ GradeForge AI X

**Your Personal AI Academic Operating System**
*Track. Learn. Improve. Dominate.*

GradeForge AI X is a complete, AI-powered academic command center that runs entirely in your browser — no installs, no accounts, no subscriptions, 100% private.

---

## 🚀 Quick Start (30 seconds)

1. **Unzip** the download (if zipped).
2. **Double-click `index.html`** — it opens in your browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox).
3. Follow the welcome screen: enter your name, pick **🌍 Global** or **🇿🇦 South Africa** mode.
4. Choose **"Explore with demo data"** to see everything working instantly, or **"Launch"** to start fresh.

That's it. Your Academic OS is online.

> 💡 **Tip:** Bookmark the page or pin the tab — your data saves automatically on your device every time you change anything.

## 📱 Use it on your phone

Open `index.html` in your phone's browser (or host the folder anywhere) — the entire OS is mobile-optimized with a bottom navigation bar.

## 🇿🇦 South Africa Mode

Switch it on in Settings (or during onboarding) to unlock:
- CAPS subject picker (Mathematics → EGD)
- **Automatic APS score** calculated live from your subject averages
- Matric finals countdown
- CAPS-aligned AI study plan generator

## 🔐 Your data

Everything is stored locally in your browser (`localStorage`). Nothing is uploaded anywhere. Use **Settings → Data Vault → Export Backup** to move your data between devices or keep a safe copy.

⚠️ Clearing your browser data erases the app's data — export a backup first.

## ❓ FAQ

**Do I need the internet?** Only the first load fetches fonts; everything else works fully offline.

**Does the AI coach need an API key?** No. The coach engineers expert-level prompts; you paste them into the AI you already use (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — free tiers work great).

**Can parents check progress?** Yes — the Command Center is a one-glance progress report.
