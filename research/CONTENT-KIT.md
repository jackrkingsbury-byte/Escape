# NeverMiss Content Kit — 10 videos, ready to record

*Your entire marketing job for the next 2–3 weeks. No cold messages. Record on
your iPad/phone, post natively to TikTok + Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts.
One video per day or every other day. Reply to every comment; the link in bio
goes to the site → demo → WhatsApp.*

**Before recording anything:** add the Anthropic key so `/demo` replies live.
Every script below leans on the demo actually answering.

**Filming setup (all of these):** iPad/phone screen-record the site or demo,
then add a voiceover or text overlays in TikTok/CapCut. Face optional. Keep
every video 20–40 seconds. First line ON SCREEN within 1 second — that's the
hook that decides everything.
